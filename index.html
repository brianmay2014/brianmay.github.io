About

Ever since I was young I have always loved to build things. It started with Legos early on, then into music, and now I am embracing the creativity that coding has to offer. My parents have their Masters in Computer Science so it was always around and I always had an interest in coding. My mind just works in solving problems, so engineering was an easy and natural choice for me to study in college.

After I got my Bachelors and Masters in Electrical Engineering, I went on to work as a Senior Engineer at Samsung for almost ten years. Near the end of my time there I made an app using Flask that reignited my coding passion, it felt so great to have an idea and see it through from the start to the finish. I was feeling stuck in my career there and the opportunities to contribute creatively and feel like I was noticed for my contributions.

I had many close friends do boot camps, and knew that it was the right step for me and my engineering career. Throughout App Academy I have sharpened my skills in Python and JavaScript, and made four applications from the ground up. By the way, check out my awesome work. I have earnestly loved learning about Redux and Flask as it gives a deeper understanding of how applications are built and built efficiently. I have really loved React and the flexibility it offers, and making the front-end of the application really stand out, while being subtle.

In my decade of engineering experience, I have learned how to effectively communication and be an engaged listener. I strongly feel the most important thing about a constructive work environment is communication. I am looking forward to changing my adjective in my engineering career to software and start building incredible applications! I love all things design, architecture, and am always reading (listening) to a book on a new topic. My other passions outside of coding include playing guitar, snowboarding, comedy, and traveling.
